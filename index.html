Privacy Policy for AeroDash

Last updated: 28/07/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AeroDash, developed by Flying Lemon Studios, collects, uses, and shares information when you play the Game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Local Data Storage

  • High scores: Stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs.
  • GDPR Consent Preference: Your consent choice for personalized ads, stored locally using Unity PlayerPrefs

1.2. Advertising & Analytics Data

We use Unity LevelPlay for ad mediation, which may collect the following information via its third-party ad networks (ironSource, Unity Ads, Google AdMob). For information on data collection by our third‑party ad networks, please refer to their privacy policies listed in Section 3.

No other personal or gameplay data is collected or transmitted by the Game.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Functionality: To store and display your high scores and ad consent choice.
  • Personalized Advertising: To deliver relevant ads through Unity LevelPlay mediation.
  • Stability & Monitoring: To diagnose and fix crashes via SDK reports.

4. Data Retention

We retain only the data stored locally on your device. Third‑party networks retain ad interaction records according to their own policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

AeroDash is not directed towards children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Reset Ad ID: You can limit or reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
  • Consent Management: You can change your ad consent preference through the Game’s settings menu.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect data stored locally on your device. We do not store any data on our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of major changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
